Frequently Asked Questions

Our sanctuary is open for scheduled pickups and visits from Monday to Friday (9:00 AM - 4:00 PM) and Saturday (10:00 AM - 2:00 PM). We are closed on Sundays to give our staff and animals a day of rest.

We are proudly located in San Mateo, Rizal, Philippines. Exact sanctuary directions will be provided in your approval email to ensure the security and privacy of our rescues.

  1. Create an Account: Register on our portal to begin your journey.
  2. Select Companions: Use "Add to List" if you wish to apply for multiple pets in one unified application, or click "Adopt Now" to proceed immediately with a single pet.
  3. Submit Application: Fill out the formal application, answer our questionnaires. Then upload a clear copy of your valid Government ID and home environment photos.
  4. Review Period: Wait for our staff to review your application. You will receive an email notification, and your Adopter Dashboard will update to reflect an "Approved" or "Declined" status.
  5. Schedule Pickup: Once approved, log in to your dashboard to select an available claiming date and time.
  6. Claim Your Pet: Visit the shelter at your scheduled time. You must bring your physical valid ID for final verification before taking your new companion home!
